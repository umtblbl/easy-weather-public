Privacy Policy for Easy Weather

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

Easy Weather ("Easy Weather", "the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Location Information

Easy Weather may request access to your device's precise or approximate location to provide accurate weather forecasts and weather-related information for your area.

Location information is used solely to:

  • Determine local weather conditions
  • Display weather forecasts relevant to your location
  • Provide weather alerts and weather-related features
  • Improve the accuracy of weather information shown in the App

Location information is only processed when you grant permission and choose to use location-based features.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Easy Weather does not require user registration and does not collect or store:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Payment information
  • Government identification information
  • Health information
  • User-generated profiles

3. How We Use Information

Any information processed by Easy Weather is used exclusively to provide weather-related functionality requested by the user.

We do not use information for:

  • Advertising
  • Marketing
  • Behavioral profiling
  • Selling personal information
  • Personalized advertising
  • Cross-app tracking

4. Data Sharing

To provide weather forecasts, Easy Weather may send location coordinates or selected location information to third-party weather data providers.

Such information is transmitted solely for the purpose of retrieving weather data and is not used by Easy Weather for advertising, analytics, profiling, or marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage

Easy Weather does not store your precise location information on its own servers.

Location information is processed temporarily and only for the duration necessary to retrieve and display weather information.

6. Permissions

Easy Weather may request the following permissions:

  • Precise Location: Used to provide weather information for your exact location.
  • Approximate Location: Used to provide weather information for your general area.

You may grant, deny, or revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Easy Weather may use third-party weather service providers to retrieve forecast data. These providers may receive location coordinates or selected location information only as required to return weather data requested by the user.

Easy Weather does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

8. Children's Privacy

Easy Weather is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your Choices

You may choose whether to share your location by:
